ABOUT

Myself

My Name is Evan Tahair, I am a 3rd-year Global Business and Digital Arts student at the University of Waterloo - Stratford Campus. As a student of Global Business and Digital Arts, I am able to bridge the gap between design, business, and technology and help deliver effective and engaging digital experiences.

Program courses include User Experience Design, Digital Media Design and Production, Digital Imaging Online Applications, Working in Teams and Project Management, Marketing in the Digital World and Global Development and Business. As a result I am well-versed in Business and Computer Science but my focus is UX/UI design. Through my education, I have developed my critical-thinking, communication and time-management skills, as well as technical skills in Adobe Creative Suite, Invision, HTML, CSS and JavaScript.

I am currently interested in co-op internships for this summer from May to August 2020. You can contact me by emailing me or submitting a form below.
