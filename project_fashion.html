Fast Fashion

The Objective

The objective was to make a documentary about a contemporary economic development issue. The documentary should be able to address the current state of the issue, as well as the impact the issue is having on economic development in society and global interconnectedness.

The Documentary

My group chose to look into the issue of fast fashion and its effects on people and the environment. Our documentary was shown at the Stratford Trashion Week event, April 19-25, 2020.

My Roles

My main role in this project was sound recordist for both the interviews and the narration. I also acted in various supporting roles throughout the process.
