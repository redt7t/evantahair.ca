The Problem

The problem was renting equipment and project rooms on the University of Waterloo's Stratford campus. At the time there was no way to reserve the project rooms, and to rent equipment you had to go to the equipment room. There was no way of reserving ahead of time or knowing if there were any cameras available to rent before you got there.

Iterative Process

My group's solution was to create a mobile application that handled renting and reserving the rooms and equipment on campus. The iterative process for this project involved initial sketches, a low-fidelity wireframe, user testing and a high-fidelity prototype.

My Roles

My work on this first-year project includes the initial sketches, low-fidelity wireframes (created in Balsamiq) and Invision prototype. I was involved in the user-testing in an equal group capacity and co-created the high-fidelity artboards in Adobe XD.
