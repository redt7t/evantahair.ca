The Objective

The objective of this group project was to identify a problem that we have experienced which could be solved by a mobile application or website. We had to prototype a solution from low-fidelity concepts to high-fidelity mockups to a working Invision prototype. We were not required to prototype the full application, simply the core functionality.

The Problem

The problem we chose to solve was renting equipment and project rooms on the University of Waterloo's Stratford campus. At the time there was no way to reserve the project rooms, they were simply first come, first serve. To rent equipment such as cameras, you had to go to the equipment room. There was no way of reserving it ahead of time or knowing if they had any cameras available to rent before you got there.

The Solution

My group's solution was to create a mobile application that handles renting and reserving the rooms and equipment on campus. The app uses a cart system that will be familiar to users, allowing them to rent multiple items at a time. Users can see live room and equipment availability as well as images, descriptions, specifications and related products for all equipment to help the user choose the correct equipment for their needs. If they are still uncertain, there are help options to connect them with the equipment staff on campus.
